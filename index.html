Frequently Asked Questions

Is Backyard Bandwidth reliable?

Absolutely! We offer a 99.99% uptime guarantee backed by 2Gbps fiber internet, armed security, and 24/7 security camera monitoring. Plus, we're WISP certified—our reliability is trusted even by the IRS!

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide responsive email support for all our services, along with weekly updates to keep you informed about your account and service status.

Tell me about the VPS?

Our VPS services are created on demand, meaning no VPS is provisioned until the first month's payment is received. The only detail we log is the VPS name. Once the VPS is terminated, all related logs and information are permanently deleted. We do not, and will never, retain access to any data, ensuring complete privacy and security.
