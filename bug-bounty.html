Backyard Bandwidth Bug Bounty Program

Privacy is our foundation. Help us keep it strong.

Scope

We reward high-impact vulnerabilities affecting critical Backyard Bandwidth infrastructure. The following domains are currently in-scope:

  • backyardbandwidth.com
  • api.backyardbandwidth.com

All other domains, systems, and assets are considered out-of-scope.

Program Rules

  • Only impactful, real-world vulnerabilities will be considered for rewards.
  • Each submission must include steps to reproduce, affected systems, and a clear explanation of impact.
  • Automated tools are permitted, provided they do not disrupt or degrade service availability.
  • Do not engage in denial-of-service, brute-force, or attacks on systems you do not own.
  • Only the first valid report of a vulnerability is eligible for a reward.
  • The following are not eligible for rewards:
  • The following are not eligible for rewards:
    • Reflected XSS without demonstrable impact
    • Clickjacking with no sensitive action
    • Missing HTTP headers unless exploitable
    • Generic or scanner-generated results without validation
    • CAPTCHA bypasses unless they lead to direct, high-impact exploitation (thank you to Bipin for bringing this to our attention)
    • Open redirects without sensitive impact
    • Missing SPF/DKIM/DMARC or email best practices
    • Disclosure of non-sensitive, public, or low-value information
    • Self-XSS that requires the victim to paste code into the console
    • Reports of outdated libraries without a proven exploit path
    • Click-through SSL/TLS warnings or weak ciphers with no exploit chain
    • Login/logout CSRF without sensitive action impact
    • Vulnerabilities only exploitable on outdated browsers or platforms
    • Denial-of-service via large payloads without service degradation
  • All reward determinations are final and based on severity, impact, and originality.

CVSS scores and vectors are appreciated, but not required.

We are unable to issue rewards to individuals or entities located in sanctioned countries or on government watchlists.

Out-of-Scope Submissions

  • Social engineering, phishing, or physical attacks
  • Denial-of-service or rate-limiting tests
  • Reports that are speculative or not reproducible
  • Legally prohibited activities

Safe Harbor

We value responsible disclosure and will not pursue legal action against security researchers acting in good faith and in accordance with this policy. If you accidentally access sensitive data, do not store, use, or share it — report it to us immediately.

Disclosure Policy

Please keep all bug reports confidential until we have acknowledged and remediated the issue. Our typical patch timeline is within 15 business days of acknowledgment.

Rewards

Reward amounts are based on severity, impact, and originality. In general:

  • Low impact: $50–$150
  • Moderate: $200–$500
  • High: $750–$1,000+

As a lean operation, some rewards may be issued as free service credits (VPN, VPS, or S3) at equivalent value. If a cash reward is promised and you’re eligible, we’ll work to honor it. However, please understand that budget constraints can sometimes delay or limit cash payouts.

All valid reports are recognized on our Wall of Fame (anonymity optional).
