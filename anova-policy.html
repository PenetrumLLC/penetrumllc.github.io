Stateless LLM – No Logs Policy

Last updated: August 19th, 2025

This No Logs Policy ("Policy") describes how our Stateless LLM service operates with respect to privacy, data handling, and liability. By interacting with our LLM through the UI or API, you acknowledge and accept the terms of this Policy.

1. No Prompt Logging

Our Stateless LLM does not log, store, or persist user prompts. Specifically:

  • User prompts are sent directly to /dev/null after processing.
  • All prompts and responses are held only in volatile memory (RAM) while the interaction occurs.
  • All session data is wiped from memory immediately after a response is delivered.
  • There are no backups or shadow logs of any interactions.

2. Stateless by Design

Our LLM is stateless. This means:

  • It has no ability to remember past conversations across sessions.
  • Each request is processed in isolation.
  • There is no continuity or memory between different user interactions.

3. Accuracy and Verification

While we strive for accurate and useful responses, the LLM can make mistakes. Users should:

  • Independently verify all information provided.
  • Not rely solely on responses for critical, financial, medical, legal, or security-related decisions.

4. Liability Disclaimer

By using our LLM, you acknowledge and accept:

  • We are not liable for any outcomes, damages, or issues arising from reliance on the LLM’s responses.
  • All responsibility rests with the user when acting on advice or information provided by the LLM.
  • You are the responsible party when interacting with the LLM via our UI or API.

5. No Third-Party Tracking

We do not integrate third-party analytics, trackers, or data collection tools into the LLM. Your interactions are private and not shared with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics platforms.

6. Acceptance of This Policy

By continuing to use the LLM, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Policy. If you do not agree, you must discontinue use immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this No Logs Policy at any time. Updates will be posted to this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
