This Fair Use Policy ("Policy") sets forth the acceptable use guidelines for Backyard Bandwidth's VPN, VPS, and storage services ("Service"). Backyard Bandwidth provides VPN services without data caps or speed restrictions. However, this offering is contingent upon fair and reasonable use. Excessive or abnormal usage that negatively impacts network performance, degrades service quality for other users, or places an undue burden on our infrastructure may result in action under this Policy.
